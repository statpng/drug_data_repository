# Indonesia Drug Report: Key Statistical Indicators (2018-2023)

This repository summarizes the consistent statistical indicators extracted from the Indonesia Drug Reports published by the National Narcotics Board (BNN). The data is categorized into **Demand Reduction** and **Supply Reduction**, focusing on metrics that are consistently tracked across multiple years.

## 1. Demand Reduction Indicators

This section covers data related to prevention, rehabilitation, and community empowerment. The most consistent data points involve the demographics of rehabilitation patients and prevalence surveys.

### A. Rehabilitation Patient Demographics
Data regarding drug abusers treated at BNN Rehabilitation Centers, Rehabilitation Halls, and Rehabilitation Houses.

* Total Drug Abusers by Gender: Consistent tracking of patients by gender.
* Total Drug Abusers by Age Group: Breakdown of patients by specific age groups.
* Total Drug Abusers based on Education: Classification based on the patient's last education level.
* Total Drug Abusers based on Occupation: Classification based on the patient's profession/job.
* Types of Drugs Consumed: Data on the specific types of drugs used by patients entering rehabilitation.

### B. Prevalence & Screening
Indicators related to the spread of drug abuse and detection efforts.

* Prevalence Rate According to Province: Drug abuse prevalence rates mapped by province.
* Total of Urine Tests by Province: The number of urine tests conducted by the Deputy of Community Empowerment in each province.
* Drug Prone Areas (Regions): Identification and count of areas classified as vulnerable to drug abuse.
* Self-Reporting to IPWL: Number of drug abusers voluntarily reporting to Institutions for Compulsory Reporting (IPWL).

## 2. Supply Reduction Indicators

This section covers law enforcement activities, including criminal cases, suspect arrests, and evidence seizures.

### A. Drug Crime Cases
* Data of Drug Crime Cases by Province: The number of drug criminal cases recorded in each province.
* Number of Drug Cases by Drug Classification: Cases categorized by the class of drugs involved.
* Rating of Successful Disclosure (Uncovered Cases): Ranking of regions based on the success rate of uncovering drug cases.

### B. Suspect Demographics
Detailed demographic data on individuals suspected of drug crimes.

* Total Suspects based on Nationality: Breakdown of suspects by Indonesian citizens vs. foreign nationals.
* Total Suspects based on Gender: Number of male and female suspects.
* Total Suspects based on Age Group: Suspects categorized by age.
* Total Suspects based on Education Level: Suspects categorized by their educational background.
* Total Suspects based on Profession: Suspects categorized by their occupation.

### C. Evidence & Seizures
Data on the quantity and type of illicit drugs seized by authorities.

* Number of Evidence in Each Province: Total evidence collected broken down by region.
* Total Seizures of Cannabis: Total amount of cannabis seized.
* Total Seizures of Narcotics: Total amount of general narcotics (including Methamphetamine/Shabu, Ecstasy, etc.) seized.
* Total Seizures of Psychotropic Substances: Total amount of psychotropic drugs seized.

### D. Financial Crimes
* Money Laundering (TPPU): Data on Money Laundering cases handled by BNN, including suspect details.
